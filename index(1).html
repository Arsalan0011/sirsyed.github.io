About Us

About

We are living in the age of innovation and technology. To keep pace with the modern world, we have introduced a state-of-the-art student portal to maintain records for each student and to provide updated information to parents regarding their students’ performance, attendance and results.

Mission

M

To play a leading role as a school & college in national socio-economic development meeting the challenges of modern time by creating alliances and partnerships between academia and industry. To focus on the studies with the heart, a sense of social and moral responsibility and imbued with the Aligarh spirit.

Inspiration

I

Sir Syed School & College is spiritually associated to Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, India. The role of Muslim School Aligarh, in the process of uplift and well being of the Muslims of the subcontinent, stands in bold relief in the annals of history. The domain of working of this university was not confined to teaching of some subjects or dissemination of knowledge; it rather transcended these spheres and encompassed the building of character and development of personality of the students along with academics.

Vision

V

SSS&C vision is to meet the demands of challenging times as envisioned by Sir Syed Ahmed Khan by transforming Pakistan into a Knowledge based society through social impact projects and innovation in education, research and creativity, endeavoring to create an entrepreneurial mindset.
